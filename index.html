1. Skill-share project (general idea) This means a collaborative project where people exchange skills. Person A teaches Person B a skill (e.g., coding). Person B teaches Person A another skill (e.g., video editing). Sometimes done in communities, colleges, or team-building workshops to encourage knowledge sharing. Example:In a group, one person good at Python can train others, while another who knows Data Visualization can share that.
